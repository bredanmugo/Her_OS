Welcome to HerOS

HerOS (Humanitarian Empowerment and Resilience Operations System) is an integrated platform designed to provide gender-responsive support during and after climate-related disasters. Utilizing NASA’s real-time data, HerOS offers disaster prediction, safe evacuation routes, and access to secure shelters, prioritizing the safety of women and children.

Our Vision

To empower and protect women and children during disasters through advanced, real-time digital support systems.

Our Mission

HerOS is committed to delivering safe, reliable, and essential resources to vulnerable populations, with a strong emphasis on inclusivity, safety, and continuity in times of crisis.

Our Goals

  • Provide timely, accurate disaster predictions to minimize risk.
  • Ensure safe evacuation routes are accessible for women and children.
  • Connect users to crucial support services including GBV hotlines and healthcare.
  • Support educational continuity through digital classrooms during disruptions.
  • Collect gender-disaggregated data to inform inclusive and effective policymaking.

Q1: How does HerOS provide disaster prediction updates?

Q1: How does HerOS determine safe evacuation routes?
